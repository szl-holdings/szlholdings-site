A worm crossed from ordinary networks into industrial controllers because, once past the perimeter, components implicitly trusted one another.

The SZL response

The Zero-Trust Mesh: a default-deny matrix where nothing talks to anything unless explicitly allowed, one reviewable rule at a time, encrypted end to end. Lateral movement has nowhere to go.

Capability: default-deny service mesh · policy matrix reviewable line by line
